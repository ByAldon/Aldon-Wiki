# How to remove a plugin

**If you don't want that plugin anymore it is best to delete the plugin.**<br>
Here is how.

---

1. Goto your plugins
<img src="assets/exclusive-tutorials/wordpress/how-to-remove-a-plugin/1.jpg">
<br><br>
2. Pick the plugin you want to get rid of and hit deactivate
<img src="assets/exclusive-tutorials/wordpress/how-to-remove-a-plugin/2.jpg"><br>
The plugin will no longer work right now.
<br><br>
3. If you want to delete your plugin, just hit "Delete". Remember doing this will permently delete the plugin.
<img src="assets/exclusive-tutorials/wordpress/how-to-remove-a-plugin/3.jpg">
